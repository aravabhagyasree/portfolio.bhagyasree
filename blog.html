Understanding Large Language Models: The Technology Reshaping AI

Large Language Models (LLMs) have rapidly evolved from research curiosities to powerful tools that millions of people use every day.

What Are Large Language Models?

LLMs are artificial intelligence systems trained on massive amounts of text data to understand and generate human language.

How Do LLMs Work?

LLMs use a neural network architecture called transformers that process language using contextual relationships between words.

Key Capabilities

They can write code, analyze data, translate languages, and generate creative text.

Limitations

LLMs may hallucinate information and rely on statistical patterns rather than real understanding.

Conclusion

LLMs are transforming how humans interact with computers and access knowledge.
